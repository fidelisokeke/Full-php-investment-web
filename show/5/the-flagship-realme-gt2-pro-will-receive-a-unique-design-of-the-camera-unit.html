Dec 5, 2021 15:58 The flagship Realme GT2 Pro will receive a unique design of the camera unit

It looks like the back panel is made of ceramic composite materials, and the frame is metal. As for the technical characteristics, insiders agree on a 32 MP front-facing camera, a powerful Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen1 processor and a 5000 mAh battery with fast charging support (according to some reports, up to 125 watts).
 
There is no consensus about the screen yet, but, according to the source, the owner of the smartphone will be able to boast a 6.8-inch OLED display with a resolution of WQHD+ and a refresh rate of 120 Hz.
 
 
The announcement of the device is expected in China in the first half of 2022. The manufacturer should not delay entering the international market.
